Interesting Facts About Honeybees

Honeybees communicate with one another by dancing. A bee's dance can tell its hive mates where to find food.

Honeybees are the only insect that produces food eaten by humans.

Beehives are composed of hexagon-shaped cells and can hold around 20,000 to 80,000 bees.

The average worker bee lives for just five to six weeks. During this time, they'll produce about 1/12th of a teaspoon of honey.

Honeybees fly at a speed of around 15 miles per hour and beat their wings 200 times per second!
